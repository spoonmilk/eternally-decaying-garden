Your idle scrolling eventually takes you to a link embedded in a message you have some faint remembrance of.

Some old P2P file services, MP3s from your friends, sharing pirated music without any care for security.

You click on the link and find yourself at the landing page for some digital storefront, offering you the name ‘cooltunes.io’ for 30 dollars a year. 

Everything once hosted here is lost to you, 

probably some time ago when whichever lonely developer who ran this server didn’t care to pay the renewal fee.

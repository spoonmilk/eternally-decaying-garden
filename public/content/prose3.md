Scrolling through these old digital haunts, you can’t help but feel you’re intruding on something.

Staring into a soul is a very naked thing, even if it’s your own. 

And as you flip through old stories and captions, you also can’t help but feel the pit of loss in your stomach. 

And with that loss, jealousy. 

This thing, this camera and phone and website and company, all remember so much better than you do. 

Why can’t you have a camera behind your eyes?
